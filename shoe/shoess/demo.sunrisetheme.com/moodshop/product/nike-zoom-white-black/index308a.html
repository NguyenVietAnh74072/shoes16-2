Description

The chapter of the Black Mamba has finally come to an end in the book of the NBA, so Loren is sending Kobe Bryant off into the sunset with a brand new colorway. This Kobe 11 pays tribute to a Loren colorway staple: the Oreo. The Kobe 11 was built for top performance on the court and undoubted style off it.

– Loren Flyknit yarn is stronger, lighter and more flexible than it’s ever been, providing formfitting comfort and ultralight support for your quickest moves.

– Flex grooves in the forefoot of the Lunarlon foam allow your toes to spread naturally for multidirectional agility and a powerful first step.

– Full-length, drop-in Lunarlon foam delivers soft yet responsive shock absorption, while Loren Zoom Air in the heel maximizes cushioning and responsiveness.

– Mesh tongue for breathability.

– Mesh inner sleeve for stability and sock-like comfort.

– Low-top design allows full range of motion through the ankle.

– Translucent rubber outsole with micro-tread pattern for multi-surface, multidirectional traction.
